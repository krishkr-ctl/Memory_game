📖 How to Play

Two Player Mode

  1. Take Turns: Players alternate turns. Player 1 starts first.
  2. Flip Cards: Click on any card to reveal the emoji hidden behind it.
  3. Find Matches: Try to match two cards with the same emoji.
  4. Earn Points: When you find a match, you score 1 point and get another turn.
  5. Pass Your Turn: If the cards don't match, both cards flip back and it becomes your opponent's turn.
  6. Win the Game: The player with the most matched pairs when all cards are revealed wins!

AI Mode

  1. Challenge the AI: Play against a smart AI opponent with memory and strategic thinking.
  2. Your Turn: Click cards to make matches and score points.
  3. AI's Turn: Watch as the AI plays smartly, remembering card positions!
  4. Difficulty: The AI gets smarter as more cards are revealed.

💡 Tip: Remember the card positions to make better matches on your next turn!
